Steps to show images from a GitHub repo:

  1. Connect your stackblitz app to your github repository
  2. From github, drop your image into your repo, allow it to upload, commit
  3. Back at stackblitz, refresh your app
  4. Click the image in the stackblitz file explorer, it will open with the message: "Nothing to see here, you can..." and a button to download the image. Click the button, the image will open in a new tab.
  5. Grab the url from the new tab, use it as the src for your image in the stackblitz app
